🚄 About Rapid Food

Rapid Food is a bilingual student-oriented food media platform that helps students find affordable and delicious meals near MRT stations across Kuala Lumpur and Selangor. We focus on meals under RM20 that are within a 5–10 minute walk from the nearest MRT station — making food discovery simple, practical, and budget-friendly for everyday commuters.

🎯 Mission

To make affordable, local meals more accessible to students and MRT commuters.

To connect students through authentic food experiences and real recommendations.

🌟 Vision

To become Malaysia’s most trusted MRT-based food guide for students.

To inspire young people to explore local food culture affordably and conveniently.
